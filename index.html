Important Information for Parents/Guardians

  • Test Results: Assessment results will be available through the parent portal approximately 4-6 weeks after testing completion
  • Make-up Testing: Students absent during testing windows will be scheduled for make-up sessions
  • Test Preparation: Resources are available through district websites and classroom teachers
  • Accommodations: Students with IEPs, 504 plans, or English Language Learners are entitled to appropriate accommodations
  • Questions: Contact your school's test coordinator or guidance office
